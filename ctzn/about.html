ABOUT

The Paris-based collective, led by YHawks and Sélimo has made a name for itself with powerful and bold aesthetics, and introducing some of the French capital’s most prominent electronic acts since 2011.

LEYY, T-O, YHawks, Lil'Kim, Sélimo, have all debuted and evolved hand-in-hand with CTZN, thus infusing their own genres and sensibilities into the label’s catalog. Consequently, CTZN stands more for an attitude rather than for a musical style in itself, with a wide-open set of genres and influences : from YHawks’s experimental excursions rooted in “musique concrète” to Sélimo’s purest ambient drones.

In 2016, the family is growing, as the label will announce a handful of ambitious projects, among which : a double album by LEYY, a collaborative double EP by T-O and Lil'Kim, a double EP by Sélimo, and new projects by Leyy and YHawks, with remixes by Peder Mannerfelt, Abdulla Rashim, and Polar Inertia.
